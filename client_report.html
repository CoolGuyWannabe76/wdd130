Client Report

I didn't really think very much when deciding on what content and images to use for my site plan. I didn't give any specific direction to my client for which images to use, and I also just told him to use filler text for most of the site.

I had good communication with my developer. There weren't really any issues.

My developer honestly exceded my expectations for my site. He did a fantastic job.

Developer Report

Communication with my client went pretty well for the most part. The only problem that I really had was that sometimes my partner would take a day to respond, but it was never longer than that, and he always provided good feedback.

I feel I handeled the uploading of my client's site well. It was just an easy upload onto github using github desktop. I didn't encounter any issues.
